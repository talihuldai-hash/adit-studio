The Studio

ADIT is a film studio working with the defense and aerospace industries, led by Yadin Deloya and Tali Huldai.

We produce capability films and system presentations for air, land, naval and intelligence platforms. Clients include ELTA, Israel Aerospace Industries and The Armored Group.

The work starts with your material — drawings, specifications, reference images — and from there the scenario is built: the environment, the time of day, the conditions and the mission. Every film is built in the studio, from brief to delivery, with no field production and no logistics.

The result is a film that works at trade shows, in sales presentations and across digital channels — accurate to your platform, not to a generic version of it.
